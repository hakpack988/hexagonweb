About Us

Welcome to The Yard Sports Development, LLC – the premier destination for baseball and softball enthusiasts seeking to elevate their game. Situated in the heart of Middletown, Ohio, at 3711 Commerce Drive, our state-of-the-art facility is the brainchild of sports visionaries OV Hakanson & Chet Hakanson, who established this center of excellence in 2023.

At The Yard Sports Development, we believe in providing our athletes with only the finest equipment to hone their skills. Our array of advanced pitching machines—including two junior Hack Attack machines, one I-Pitch machine, and three Fungoman machines—ensures a diverse and challenging training environment. Furthermore, our facility boasts two Hittrax machines, complete with all the necessary components to analyze and improve your batting performance.

Our commitment to technology and analytics extends to our integration of the Rapsodo 3.0 system, offering precise metrics for players and coaches to refine pitching and hitting tactics with unprecedented accuracy.

The cornerstone of our developmental approach lies in the expertise of our well-respected instructors. Their wealth of experience and knowledge empowers our athletes, driving them towards their full potential. We pride ourselves on not only nurturing talent but also fostering a community where instruction and growth go hand in hand.

At Hexagon, we understand that improvement is a continuous journey. That's why we offer an array of camps and clinics designed to provide athletes with intensive training opportunities to sharpen their abilities, learn new strategies, and stay ahead of the competition.

We extend our passion for baseball and softball beyond the diamond through our online merchandise store, providing a curated selection of gear and apparel for our dedicated athletes and fans alike.

Stay connected with us and the broader community of The Yard Sports Development through our informative newsletter, your source for the latest news, tips, and highlights from within the ranks of our growing talent pool.

Join us at The Yard Sports Development, LLC, where dedication to the sport meets cutting-edge technology and expert guidance, all in pursuit of the great American pastime.
