Yard Experience Guardian

Company Overview:
The Yard Sports Development LLC, a premier baseball and softball training facility, opened its doors in 2023. Located off Interstate 75 at the Middletown exit, our 11,000 sf state-of-the-art facility is equipped with cutting-edge technology including 2 HitTrax systems and Rapsoto 3.0. Our commitment to utilizing technology to enhance athletic performance is matched by the expertise of our instructors, who are adept at translating complex data into actionable insights for individual performance improvement.

Job Summary:
As an Experience Guardian at The Yard, you will be the heart of our client engagement, ensuring an exceptional experience both on the floor and at the front desk. This dual role requires a proactive, engaging individual dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of customer service and facility operation. Your responsibilities range from fine-tuning and calibrating pitching machines to managing front desk operations, including handling bookings and inquiries. The ideal candidate will be enthusiastic, detail-oriented, and passionate about delivering a seamless experience to every client.
Key Responsibilities:

  • Engage actively with clients to ensure a welcoming and enjoyable environment.
  • Familiarize yourself with all membership options and effectively answer client questions.
  • Calibrate and tune pitching machines to ensure they are in perfect working order.
  • Monitor the facility and assist in troubleshooting any issues, ensuring a seamless experience for clients.
  • Maintain order and safety in the batting cages and open areas.
  • Coordinate with the front desk to confirm client cage assignments and manage facility flow.
  • Ensure all guests have signed the required waiver upon entry.
  • Operate our booking system proficiently, managing schedules and client information.
  • Maintain cleanliness of tables, chairs, and surrounding areas to ensure a tidy and welcoming environment.
  • Handle customer inquiries and provide business information over the phone with professionalism.
  • Maintain high levels of customer satisfaction through excellent service.
  • Promptly and professionally resolve any issues that arise with customers.

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, and customer service skills.
  • Strong attention to detail and problem-solving abilities.
  • Experience in customer service and sales
  • Prior experience in a front desk or hospitality role
  • A passion for sports and an understanding of baseball or softball.
  • A positive outlook and ability to collaborate well within a team.
  • Responsible, punctual, and deeply committed to fulfilling all job responsibilities.
Job Perks:
         • The Yard Logo T-Shirts.
         • Discounts on services at The Yard, including Hexagon Muscle Recovery and Peppers World Café.
         • Reduced prices on sports equipment from The Yard Pro Shop.
         • Opportunity for bonus compensation based on performance.
