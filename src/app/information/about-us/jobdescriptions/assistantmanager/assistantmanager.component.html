Assistant Manager

Company Overview:
The Yard Sports Development LLC, a premier baseball and softball training facility, opened its doors in 2023. Located off Interstate 75 at the Middletown exit, our 11,000 sf state-of-the-art facility is equipped with cutting-edge technology including 2 HitTrax systems and Rapsoto 3.0. Our commitment to utilizing technology to enhance athletic performance is matched by the expertise of our instructors, who are adept at translating complex data into actionable insights for individual performance improvement.

Job Summary:
The Assistant Manager at The Yard Sports Development faciliy is a hybrid role that combines managerial responsibilities with direct client engagement. This position supports the General Manager in overseeing facility operations, managing staff, and ensuring excellent customer service, while also directly participating in floor activities like equipment calibration and client interaction. The Assistant Manager plays a critical role in maintaining operational efficiency, enhancing customer satisfaction, and contributing to the strategic growth of the facility.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in Operations Management: Support the General Manager in the daily operations of the facility. Ensure all areas, including batting cages, pitching lanes, and open areas, are well-maintained and operational.
  • Direct Client Engagement: Engage with clients directly, ensuring a welcoming and enjoyable environment. Manage front desk operations, handle bookings, and address client inquiries.
  • Staff Coordination: Assist in the recruitment, training, and management of staff members. Help schedule shifts and oversee payroll activities.
  • Customer Service Excellence: Resolve customer issues promptly and maintain high levels of client satisfaction. Ensure all clients receive outstanding service both at the front desk and in the facility.
  • Financial Tasks: Manage day-to-day expenses and contribute to financial profitability strategies.
  • Safety and Compliance: Help ensure compliance with safety standards and protocols across all facility operations.
  • Marketing and Client Relations: Assist in marketing efforts and participate in local community events to promote the facility and enhance client engagement.
  • Strategic Support: Collaborate with the General Manager in strategic planning and implementation to improve facility performance and customer experience.
  • Customer Service: Maintain high levels of customer satisfaction through excellent service.
  • Promptly and professionally resolve any issues that arise with customers.
  • Membership and Client Relations: Lead a constant membership drive to actively outreach and sign up new clients while maintaining strong relationships with existing clients, ensuring a steady growth in facility patronage.
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Sports Management, or a related field preferred.
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in management or supervisory roles within a sports facility, recreation center, or customer service environment.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a strong focus on customer service.
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks efficiently and adapt to varying work conditions.
  • A strong passion for sports, particularly baseball or softball, with an understanding of the sport’s dynamics.
  • Youth Engagement: Demonstrates enthusiasm for mentoring young athletes.
  • Character and Relationship Building: High integrity individual skilled in building relationships.
  • Communication Skills: Exceptional ability to communicate clearly and effectively.
  • Team Spirit: Positive outlook and ability to collaborate well within a team.
  • Professionalism: Responsible, punctual, and fully committed to the development of players.
