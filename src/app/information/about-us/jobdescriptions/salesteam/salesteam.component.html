The Yard - Sales Team member


Leadership. Energy. Growth. As a sales team member, you will be responsible for providing leadership and assisting the Facility Manager with delivering daily direction to the sales and group personal training teams. As part of the leadership team for the sales function, the ASM must assist with lead generation / marketing strategies to increase fitness program sales as well as promote the health and wellness benefits that are associated with a healthy lifestyle. This leader must pose a high caliber approach to accountability, collaboration, and positivity. The ASM works hand-in-hand with the Studio Manager and Head Coach to ensure excellence in customer service, operations and studio performance.
